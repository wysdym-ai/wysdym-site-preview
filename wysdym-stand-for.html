Six things we believe.

Not a values poster. The actual convictions that decide what we build — and what we refuse to.

01

The platform, not another agent.

More agents won't grow revenue. The layer beneath them will — the memory, skills, governance, and feedback loop every agent inherits.

02

Compounding beats flash.

A win you can't reuse is a demo. We judge ourselves on whether your GTM gets smarter every week — not on one shiny output.

03

Your truth, not the model's guess.

Agents act on your positioning, your playbook, your outcomes — grounded in your business, governed on your terms.

04

Trust is the product.

Read-only by default. Write-approval queues. Receipts on every action. Governance isn't the brake — it's what lets you take your hands off the wheel.

05

We rise with the models.

We're a substrate, not a competitor to the foundation models. The better they get, the better wysdym gets. We're long on progress.

06

Honest about where we are.

We tell you what's live and what's roadmap — clearly labeled, every time. Honesty and trust are how we work with every customer.
