what changed from v1 ✎

v1: five real-looking folds compacted the sheet into a packet — then the packet faded out and the finished crane popped in. Beautiful folds, but the ending was a substitution.

v2: the engine is inverted. Each fold's flap rotates over its crease and lands exactly as one facet of the crane logo — wing, wing, body, head, tail — while the flat sheet shrinks as material is consumed. Fold five sweeps the tail across, the last of the sheet disappears into it, and the thing sitting on the page is the five-facet crane itself. The metaphor closes: five components, five folds, one working agent.
