the details

Common questions.

What does it cost? +
Your first 90 days are free. If it works and you convert, you keep your early-customer pricing locked for 24 months. Nothing is charged without a countersigned order form.
How much of my time is this? +
A weekly 30-minute working session during onboarding, plus whoever needs to be in the room for your one use case. Founder-led on our side — you're not handed to a CSM.
Do I have to replace my current agents? +
No. wysdym is agent- and CRM-agnostic — it's the layer your existing agents plug into over MCP. Keep what you use; we make it compound.
Is my data safe? +
Read-only by default, per-agent permissions, write-approval queues, human-in-the-loop, and a full audit trail. Governed before it's clever.
Do I have to do co-marketing? +
Only if you want to. Some customers go public with us, some stay quiet. Both are fine.
