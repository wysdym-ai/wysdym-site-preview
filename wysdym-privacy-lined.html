the fine print ↓

Privacy, in plain English.

We build the governed layer under your agents — read-only by default, writes behind approvals, receipts for everything. We hold ourselves to the same standard with your data.

✎ draft for team review — the final policy ships with the live site
what lands on the sheet

What we collect

  • Account basics — your name, work email, and company, when you apply for access or write to us.
  • Product usage — which skills run, what gets approved or edited, and how the platform is used, so the harness can learn from outcomes.
  • Connected sources — the GTM systems your team wires in through wysdymConnect, with the permissions you set. Read-only by default.

Nothing scraped, nothing bought, nothing collected beyond what the platform needs to work.

what we do with it

How we use it

To run the harness for your team: ground your agents in your knowledge graph, execute the skills you invoke, and attribute outcomes so the whole stack gets smarter. Your graph is per-tenant — your data compounds for you, not for anyone else.

underline this part

What we never do

  • We never sell your data. To anyone. Ever.
  • We never train shared models on your tenant's knowledge graph.
  • We never write to your systems without an approval path you configured.
